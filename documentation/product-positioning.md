# Pairc - Product Positioning

## What Pairc is

Pairc is a specialist match-day GAA analyst tool.

It is designed for:

- live capture
- live interpretation
- coach communication

## Who it is for

Primary user:

- an analyst or tactically minded coach working live at a match

Secondary user:

- a coach reading the digest or acting on the analyst's read

## What job it solves

Pairc helps a team answer:

- where are kickouts going?
- are we winning them?
- where are shots coming from?
- where are turnovers happening?
- what should we tell the team now?

## What it is not

Pairc is not currently:

- a full club performance platform
- a player development system
- a season analytics warehouse
- a collaboration suite for many staff roles

## Product discipline

The product should stay strongest in:

- clarity
- speed
- trust
- live usability

Any future feature should be tested against one question:

Does this make match-day capture, live reading, or coach handoff better?
